Story

Looking down on him, I dont feel any remorse, its
almost like I enjoyed the moment. Striking my
blade through his chest. It felt awkwardly good...
I guess it was the culmination of everything that
had happened. The culmination of my training, my
efforts, my life. He used to dream of being a king,
but now he knows that was never his destiny.

His eyes finally met mine, and his jaw went
slack as his mouth opened, unable to form
words. The sound of my voice broke him, and he
stumbled against my grip, trying to regain his
feet but failing miserably. I prayed at his body
jerked and contorted, contending with my words,
my voice, my power.
